Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a habit?

Tap the "+" button in the Habits tab. You can set a name, choose an icon, and decide if you want to share it with your friends.

What are Social Circles?

Circles allow you to track shared goals with your community. Join a circle using an 8-character invite code provided by the circle admin.

How are streaks calculated?

Streaks represent consecutive days where you've completed a specific habit. Your Performance Streak represents consecutive days where ALL of your habits were completed.
